About Us

Established in 2000 by Mr. Kishan Agarwal, the visionary behind Classic Garden, Classic Convention Three, and Athidhi Convention, Classic Group stands as a beacon of excellence in event management and venue operations in Telangana State. Renowned for creating prestigious venues that epitomize premium quality in the city of Hyderabad, we are dedicated to delivering unparalleled services to the event industry.

Classic Group is a multifaceted organization with a formidable presence in government contracts, event management, and venue operations. Leveraging years of experience and a diverse portfolio, we have earned the trust of our clients in both the public and private sectors.

Our unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction propels our success across these diverse industries. At Classic Group, we strive to exceed expectations and set new standards of excellence in everything we do.

The founder, Mr. Kishan Agarwal, introduced a pioneering concept aimed at elevating the event industry by creating prestigious gatherings at Classic Venues. His vision revolves around providing individuals with the perfect setting to celebrate life's most cherished moments. Mr. Agarwal's passion extends beyond event management; he has curated a collection of vintage two-wheelers and luxury four-wheelers such as Rolls Royce, enhancing the allure and prestige of our venues such as Classic Gardens, Classic Convention Three and Athidhi Convention.
Mr. Kishan Agarwal
Taking the Classic Group to new heights is Mr. Praveen Agarwal, whose vision led to the establishment of Classic Gardens at Paradise. Renowned for his innovative approach, Mr. Agarwal has enhanced the quality of services at Classic Gardens, positioning it as a premier venue in Hyderabad. Its central location in the heart of the city further adds to its appeal. Mr. Agarwal's dedication extends beyond venue management; he actively participates in the Corporate Connections, fostering formal business relationships and advancing the company's interests in the corporate community.
Mr. Praveen Agarwal
Driving the business towards its next stage is Mr. Rakesh Agarwal, who spearheaded the integration of Classic Convention Three with Athidhi Convention. This strategic move has resulted in a combined capacity to accommodate gatherings of up to 10,000 individuals, offering unparalleled infrastructure and comprehensive end-to-end event solutions. Mr. Agarwal's proactive involvement extends to the BNI Hyderabad Chapter "Unicorn," where he actively contributes to fostering business connections and synergies within the community.
Mr. Rakesh Agarwal
